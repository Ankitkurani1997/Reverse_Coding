COMMON HINTS:
1) Fibonacci sequence: It is a sequence of numbers which follow the relation sequence:- 0,1,1,2,3,5,…
2) Pascal's triangle: It is a triangular representation of numbers in which each row corresponds to the binomial coefficients of the corresponding power of the binomial.
1
1 1
1 2 1
1 3 3 1
3) Prime numbers are those numbers which have only 2 factors, 1 and the number itself.
4) Factorial of a number n is defined as the product of all numbers from 1 to n.
eg 3!=6=1*2*3
5) Square numbers:-
The product of number multiplied by itself.
eg- 52 =25=5*5
6) Triangular no:-
Any series of numbers obtained by continuous summation of natural numbers.
eg- 1,3,6,10,15,…
2,4,7,11,16,…
7) Pallindrome number:-
A number remains same when its digits are reversed.
eg- 121, 595
8) Armstrong number:-
An Armstrong no of 3 digits is an integer such that sum of cube of its digits is equal to number itself.
eg- 371=33+73+13 =371
9) Cube numbers:-
These are the cubes of the natural nos.
eg- 8 = 2*2*2 = 23
10) Arithmetic progression:-
Sequence of nos. in which each no. differs from preceding no by a constant difference
eg.- 1,3,5,7,9,… common diff=2
11) Geometric progression:-
A progression of nos with the constant ratio between each no and preceding one before.
eg- 1,3,9,27,81,…
